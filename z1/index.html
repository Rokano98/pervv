Pizza Margarita

Margarita is considered one of the most delicious pizzas in Italy. Its composition is very simple. To make this pizza, you need tomatoes, mozzarella and basil. At the same time, its taste turns out to be unique and balanced. Some people think that pizza without bacon, sausage or meat can't be delicious, but they just haven't tried Margarita.

Neapolitan pizza

The pizza was named after the city where it was first cooked – Naples. True gourmets and connoisseurs of Italian cuisine claim that you can try real Neapolitan pizza only there, since high-quality local products are required for its preparation.

Marinara Pizza

Marinara pizza does not have to be prepared using seafood. There is a hypothesis that it was named after the fishermen who ate it every morning. The sauce made of tomatoes, olive oil, onions or garlic could be stored for a long time, which was used by seafarers.

Pizza Pugliese

This pizza is a real gift for onion lovers, because one finished pizza takes at least a quarter kilo of onions cut into half rings. Olives or capers growing in Puglia are also added to it. The taste qualities of this pizza are complemented by fresh tomatoes, olive oil and first-class hard cheese.
